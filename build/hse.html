    HSE

    Health, Safety, & Environment Policy

    Shouted HSE department continuously promotes proper safety in the work place through the implementation of our safety policy. Shouted Co. is a steel fabrication and integrated construction company, requiring a highly trained workforce, which is considered the company’s greatest asset. Shouted prides itself on its commitment to the health and safety of its team and the public, as well as implementing policies to minimise any negative impact on the environment. We implement the highest safety standards in order to minimise any risk to our team, the public, the environment, as well as equipment and private property.

    • We protect our team by:
    • Creating a safe workspace for our team
    • Surveying and identifying all health, safety and environmental hazards
    • Training our team to recognise and eliminate safety hazards and risks
    • Use of all necessary and suitable Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) when in any work area
    • Periodical review of our policy to ensure its compliance with the latest local and international safety standards
    • Periodical review of our policy to ensure its compliance with the latest local and international safety standards
    • Complying with all local laws and regulations
    • Having an HSE representative at every work area that reports directly to top management
    • We protect the public by:
    • Limiting access to our work areas to HSE trained personnel
    • Briefing visitors on our company’s HSE policy prior to work area entry
    • Providing visitors with necessary PPE
    • We protect the environment by:
    • Training our team on the importance of environmental awareness
    • Correctly disposing of all waste
    • Minimising our environmental footprint
    • Using the latest technologies in our equipment and building materials to ensure minimal environmental impact
    • Periodically training our team on their workplace health, safety, and environmental footprint

    We aim to achieve renowned regional presence as a multidisciplinary steel fabricator and integrated construction contractor
