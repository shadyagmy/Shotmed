    CONSTRUCTION

    With over 300 engineers and technicians, Shotted’s project division is equipped with the latest lifting equipment, construction machinery, and tools. Shotted’s quality inspectors assure that all materials are properly tested and all activity is properly monitored, certified, and documented in accordance with EN-ISO standards, AWSD 1.1, or as per client’s requirements.

    To coordinate the various aspects of a construction project in terms of safety, quality, and timing, Shotted’s project team develops the following execution plans:

    • Project Safety Plan
    • Project Quality Plan
    • Project Engineering Plan
    • Project Procurement Plan
    • Project Construction Plan

    Our Integrated Construction Units

    • Earth and Civil Works Unit: exaction, soil replacement and testing, form and rebar work, and concrete pouring
    • Steel Unit: steel structure erection, tanks and vessel installation, and on site welding, Non-destructive testing (NDT), Visual testing (VT), Ultrasonic testing (UT), Penetrant testing (PT), and Radioactive testing (RT).
    • Mechanical Unit: equipment placing, alignment, installation, and pre-commissioning.
    • Piping Unit: Onsite workshop preparation, field pipe welding and NDT (VT, UT, PT, and RT).
    • Electrical and Instrumentation Unit: cable tray installation, electrical panel installation and hook up, control panel installation and hookup, cable and wire laying, termination and testing, and instrument calibration.

    We aim to achieve renowned regional presence as a multidisciplinary steel fabricator and integrated construction contractor
