    Quality

    At Shotmed, quality control, consistency, and standardization are met by implementing the following policy:

    Shotmed Co. is a steel fabrication and integrated construction company, committed to ensuring that client satisfaction is achieved at every phase of a project by providing on time quality work. The Shotmed team seeks to provide quality products by ensuring that customers’ needs, project standards, material specifications, tolerances, and overall end-user requirements are met.

    • We consider the following practices to be our top priority:
    • Operating and maintaining a safe work environment
    • Ensuring that all activates and products are environmentally sustainable and “Built to Last”
    • Realising quality products by following relevant standards, specifications, and codes
    • Continually training and improving
    • Periodically reviewing our Quality Management System by determining and assessing our quality objectives
    • Striving for a sustainable product
